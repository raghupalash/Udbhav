About Us

Take a glimpse into who we are...

Our Vision

Our vision is a community where hunger no longer exists, where we breathe in a safe environment and every individual is educated enough to empower the society with the kindle of their talent.

Our Mission

We are committed to ending the plague of illiteracy, disempowerment, hunger and carelessness towards environment. We are an intergenerational community that integrates responsibility and dedication as a vehicle to connect everyone, providing social support and cultural enrichment.

Our Story

We started 6 months ago in the "City of Lakes" - Bhopal with a motive of eradication of hunger, illiteracy and other environmental issues. We have been growing gradually since then. Today we have 100+ volunteers in the city helping for the cause. We aim to spread around the state & country and make this world a better place for future generations.
