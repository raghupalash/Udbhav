What we do?

UDBHAV works for different parts of our society. We believe in improving every aspect which is in our hand. Our only motive is to create a better society. Initiatives are taken in different fields to walk a step closer to our mission.

1.

Food Distribution

There are millions of people sleeping empty stomach. We here in UDBHAV vows to fill as much stomachs as we can under or campaign H.O.P.E - Help Other People Eat.

2.

Education

Education is the right of everyone. We here believe that a good education can build the nation. We are trying our level best to educate the underprivileged in our organization under policies like Beti Padhao, Beti Bachao.

3.

Environment

We understand the need of the hour is to protect our environment. So UDBHAV has intiated a Go Greener, Breathe Cleaner! campaign under which we work for a greener city by planting trees.

4.

Empowerment

Some sections of our society needs to be empowered. Their voice need to be heard. We here in UDBHAV support their voice and help them to reach out to a responsibile authority.
